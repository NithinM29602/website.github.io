Mountain Climbing

Mountain climbing is a serious adventure sport. A mountain climber, in mountains like the Himalayas and the alps, has to make a lot of preparations before embarking on scaling a peak. Improving one's physical fitness level and stamina build up is essential. Also, acclimatization to the atmosphere at high altitudes is to be carried out. Normally, the base camps provide the initial training and conditioning for this. the climbers have to carry food, necessary equipment's like pickaxes, ropes, warm clothing, flares, etc. along with oxygen cylinders on their way up. Sherpas (Nepali ethnic groups) and helpers may bring along some of the items only up to a certain point, beyond which the climbers have to tote all that weight on their own, which calls for a lot of physical strength, stamina, will power, courage and determination. A 'never say die' attitude and team spirit are also vital. All the hard work is worthwhile, for the feeling of exhilaration one experiences when one is on the peak is beyond description.
